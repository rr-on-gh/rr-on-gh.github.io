Features

Listen & Repeat Practice

Interactive audio playback with automated timing - listen to English, translate, then hear German twice for practice.

Speaking Practice

Record your German pronunciation and receive instant AI-powered feedback on grammar and accuracy.

Multiple Difficulty Levels

Choose from A1 (beginner) to C2 (advanced) based on the Common European Framework (CEFR).

Real-World Scenarios

Practice conversations in 8 everyday situations: stores, restaurants, doctor, salon, parties, work, neighbors, and transport.

Sentence Sources

Use 350+ offline sentences from local database or generate custom AI sentences with your keywords.

Settings

Sentence Source

Choose between Local Database (default) or LLM for sentence generation.

AI Provider

When using LLM, select your preferred provider: OpenAI (default) or Bedrock.

API Keys

Enter your API keys for AI-powered features. Keys are stored securely on your device.

  • OpenAI API Key: Get from platform.openai.com
  • Bedrock API Key: Bedrock is an AWS service. You need an AWS account and can get your API key from AWS Bedrock documentation

How to Use

üí° Improve Voice Quality

This app is optimized for premium voices. Download them for the best experience: Go to Settings > Accessibility > Spoken Content > Voices. Select English and download "Zoe (Premium)", then German and download "Anna (Premium)".

Listen & Repeat Practice

  1. Tap the Listen & Repeat tab at the bottom
  2. Tap the settings icon (slider) to configure your level, scenario, keywords, and sentence count (1-20)
  3. Tap Generate Sentences to load sentences from your chosen source
  4. Tap Play to start automated playback
  5. The app will show English, pause for translation, then play German twice
  6. Tap any sentence card to reveal the English translation
  7. Tap the speaker icon on a card to hear just that sentence
  8. Tap the help icon (waveform) for voice quality tips

Speaking Practice

  1. Tap the Speaking tab at the bottom
  2. Configure your level, scenario, and keywords
  3. Grant microphone and speech recognition permissions when prompted
  4. Tap Get New Sentence to load an English sentence
  5. Read the English sentence aloud
  6. Tap Record and speak the German translation
  7. Tap Stop when done speaking
  8. Review your transcription and AI grammar feedback

Troubleshooting

Speech Recognition Not Working

  • When you first tap Record, grant microphone and speech recognition permissions when prompted
  • If permissions were denied, go to Settings > Apps > Practice German > Microphone and enable it
  • If permissions were denied, go to Settings > Apps > Practice German > Speech Recognition and enable it
  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection
  • Speak clearly and at a moderate pace
  • Try restarting the app if permissions were recently changed

LLM Not Generating Sentences

  • Verify your API key is entered correctly in Settings
  • Check that you have API credits available
  • Ensure you have an internet connection
  • Try switching to Local Database mode

Voice Quality Issues

  • Tap the help icon (waveform) in Listen & Repeat for voice setup instructions
  • Download premium voices (Zoe for English, Anna for German) from Settings > Accessibility > Spoken Content > Voices
  • Ensure you have enough storage for premium voice downloads
