Privacy Policy

1. Data Controller

Rakshith Rao | apps@rao.sh

2. Data We Collect

Usage Data: The app stores your learning progress locally on your device.

Subscription Data: When you purchase a subscription, transaction data is transmitted to our servers for verification and storage. We only store technical data (transaction IDs, subscription status) without personal information such as name or email address.

3. Purpose of Data Processing

Data processing is performed to provide app functionality, manage subscriptions, and improve the learning experience.

4. Data Sharing

Your data is not shared with third parties, except for payment processing through Apple and use of AWS services for AI features. Only the content you provide (such as keywords and practice settings) is sent to AWS to generate personalized sentences.

5. Data Storage

Subscription data is stored on AWS servers in the USA. Local data remains on your device.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, delete, and restrict the processing of your data. Contact us at apps@rao.sh.

7. Changes

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy. Changes will be announced in the app.
